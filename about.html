Our Story

Anthony ran his family's cleaning company for 20 years, servicing over 100,000 apartment turns. He lived the chaos firsthand — whiteboards, endless emails, missed schedules, and units that weren't ready on move-in day.

He brought in his longtime friend John, an engineer with deep experience in AI and technology, to build what the industry had been waiting for.

Together they're building TURN to strengthen the relationship between property managers and vendors — replacing the manual coordination that costs everyone time, money, and sanity.
