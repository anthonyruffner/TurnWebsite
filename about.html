the problem.

We spent hundreds of hours with property managers who were great at their jobs and completely buried in coordination that shouldn't exist. We kept hearing the same thing from both sides of every turn: the work is never the hard part. The chaos around it is.

The Make-Ready Process is Broken

When a resident moves out, a clock starts. Every day a unit sits vacant is revenue that doesn't come back. And yet the process of getting that unit ready still runs on whiteboards, group texts, and email chains that nobody can actually track.

Property managers are chasing vendors. Vendors are waiting on answers. Regionals are flying blind across a portfolio. And somewhere in the middle, move-in dates slip. Not because the work isn't getting done, but because nobody has a clear picture of where things stand.

"The work isn't usually the hardest part. The coordination is."

That's the problem TURN is built to solve.

Built Through Lived Experience

Most property management software is built for the person sitting at a desk. TURN was built by someone who wasn't.

Our founder Anthony spent years running vendor operations on the make-ready side of apartment turns. The cleaning crews, the last-minute schedule changes when summer move-out season hit and every unit needed work at once, the units that got bumped because a new lease came in overnight. He knew what it felt like to show up ready and get stopped by bad information. To do great work with no way to prove it.

That experience is baked into every decision TURN makes. We know what vendors need to succeed, and we know what property managers need to trust them. The product exists at that intersection.

We Build For Real Workflows

TURN is not trying to replace your property management system. We're plugging into the part of your process that already causes the most friction: vendor coordination during turns. Making it visible, trackable, and a lot less chaotic.

  • No buzzwords. We build software that reduces real coordination overhead for real teams — not "AI-powered everything."
  • Vendor-first experience. The vendor side of the make-ready process has always been an afterthought in property software. We designed TURN from the vendor's perspective out. If vendors don't adopt the tool, nothing else works.
  • Data should be a byproduct of doing the work. TURN captures what's happening automatically, so you get real insight without adding steps to your process.
  • Accountability goes both ways. TURN helps property managers see what vendors are doing, and helps vendors prove that they delivered.

Three Roles. One Shared System.

Turns involve property managers, ownership, and vendors, all working toward the same deadline from completely different vantage points. TURN connects all three.

Property managers

Schedule vendors, track every step, and run the full make-ready from one board. No more chasing confirmations.

Regionals & ownership

See turn times, unit status, and vendor performance across the full portfolio. The visibility that protects NOI.

Vendors

Get assignments, confirm dates, and message the property team from your phone.
