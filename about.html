The TURN Story

TURN exists because of the community we've built over decades of working side by side with property managers. When the idea surfaced in mid-2025, we didn't start by building software. We started by asking questions: Are you as stressed as we are? Is this process as broken as it feels? If we built something better, would you help shape it?

We spent hundreds of hours in conversation with management teams. And what we heard was consistent: everyone believed there had to be a better way, and many were ready to help create it.

As the vision became clearer, Anthony brought in his longtime friend John, who has deep experience in AI and technology, to help translate real-world turnover challenges into a platform built to support the way property managers actually work.

This wasn't built in isolation. It was built with you. To help managers like you. And vendors like us.

Built From 20 Years of Experience

TURN was born from 20 years on the vendor side of the make-ready process.

Our founder, Anthony, helps lead his family's cleaning company, which has serviced well over 100,000 apartment turns in the last two decades. We have lived the chaos: misread boards, scheduling breakdowns, delayed move-ins, and constant back-and-forth between teams and vendors.

We didn't build TURN to tweak the system.

We built it because the system doesn't work.

This platform was built to solve the problems we face every single day, alongside the property managers we have worked beside for years.

Built, informed, and tested not by "user groups," but by overworked property managers who feel like family. The Charlies. The Jessicas. The Katies. The people carrying the stress of every delayed turn and who deserve better tools.

For years, we joked about a better way and hoped someone would build it.

A decade later, we are building it ourselves, hand in hand with the teams who rely on it.

What's the Point?

It's a question we ask ourselves often. We are not hyperscaling tech gurus. We are close friends building something we believe will genuinely improve the lives of the people who use it. Our goal is simple. Fewer early mornings and late nights digging through scheduling email threads. Fewer frantic follow-ups. Less finger-crossing that a unit is ready when the inspector shows up. Scheduling breakdowns cost vendors time with family and leave property managers carrying delays they did not cause. These are pains we have lived and witnessed firsthand. And they are solvable. TURN is built for the people on the front lines, property managers and vendors navigating daily pressure and constant moving pieces. TURN only matters if it helps you. We are committed to that. Giving you your life back.
