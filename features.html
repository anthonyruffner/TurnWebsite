How a Turn Works in TURN.

01
Move-Out Logged
A unit is flagged as vacant. The turn clock starts.
02
Scope Set
Property manager builds the make-ready checklist: cleaning, paint, carpet, appliances.
03
Vendors Assigned
Each job is dispatched to the right vendor, who gets notified by email instantly.
04
Vendors Confirm
Vendors accept the job, confirm timing, and update when the work is done.
05
Completion Verified
TURN captures proof of completion and updates the board automatically.
06
Unit Cleared
Property manager sees the unit is move-in ready. Leasing can proceed.

"Every step is visible to the right person at the right time — without anyone chasing an update."

Vendor-First Design

Vendors actually use it.

Built from the vendor's side of the process — not as an afterthought.

Most property software is built for the person at a desk. TURN is designed around the vendor showing up to do the work. They receive a clean job summary by email, confirm timing directly, and mark completion when done — creating a clear record for both sides without adding friction to either.

Real-Time Alerts

Know the moment something goes off track.

No more end-of-day surprises.

TURN confirms every job with your vendor before the scheduled date. If a vendor is late, unresponsive, or declines, you're alerted immediately — with options to reassign or follow up. Problems surface in hours, not after a move-in date has already slipped.
