Real-Time Alerts

Know The Moment Something Goes Off Track.

No more end-of-day surprises.

TURN confirms every job with your vendor before the scheduled date. If a vendor is late, declines, or goes silent, you're alerted immediately with options to reassign or follow up. You find out about problems before they become delays.

Analytics

See average turn times by property, vendor on-time rates, job acceptance rates, and how long units sit vacant waiting for work. Regionals and ownership get the visibility they need across the whole portfolio, automatically, as a byproduct of the work being done.
