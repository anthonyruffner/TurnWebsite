How It Works

From Move-Out To Move-In Ready.

🏠
01
Move-Out Logged
A unit is flagged as vacant. The turn clock starts.
📋
02
Scope Set
Property manager builds the make-ready checklist for the unit.
📤
03
Vendors Assigned
Each job is dispatched to the right vendor. They get notified instantly.
✅
04
Vendors Confirm & Execute
Vendors accept the job and mark it complete from their phone.
🔍
05
Completion Verified
TURN confirms the work and updates the board automatically.
🔑
06
Unit Cleared
Property manager sees the unit is move-in ready. Leasing can proceed.
"Every step is visible to the right person at the right time, without anyone chasing an update."
